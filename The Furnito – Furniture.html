About us

We supply various kinds of hospital furniture, such as: electric hospital bed, stretchers, electric operating table, manual hospital bed, pediatric bed, automatic turning bed, hospital trolleys, and pharmacy devices. All products are strictly controlled by our quality system and all materials must be purchased from first level suppliers. All products meet ISO standard.

The products are widely applied to hospital, welfare institution and family nursing. We have been committed to providing our customers with excellent products and services for more than 5 years.

Our goal is seeking for innovation, customer satisfaction and society contribution, and becoming the leader of the health industry.
